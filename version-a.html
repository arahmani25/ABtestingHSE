Programme Overview

About the Programme

The Master's programme in Business Analytics and Big Data Systems prepares students for careers in data-driven decision making, machine learning engineering, and business intelligence at leading international organisations.

The programme is delivered entirely in English across four semesters at HSE University's Moscow campus. Students graduate with a Russian Master's degree recognised across the CIS, Europe, and increasingly in Asia and the Middle East.

Admission Requirements

Applicants must hold a Bachelor's degree in a relevant field. English proficiency is demonstrated through IELTS (6.0+), TOEFL (80+), or equivalent. Entry is through competitive selection including portfolio review and interview.
